Photography Blog

My personal photography blog.

Since I mostly love to take pictures at concerts, I can't really carry around a big DSLR camera all the time... so instead, I use a Sony DSC-RX100/B 20.2 MP Exmor CMOS Sensor digital camera with 3.6x zoom. I've never had a press pass for any of the concerts I've taken pictures at; I'm just an extremely lucky music fan who loves to go to gigs and bring my camera.

Taking pictures has been one of my favorite hobbies for as long as I can remember. Thanks to the photography class I took during my freshman year of college, I've had experience with a little bit of everything... although my favorite things to shoot are definitely landscapes and concerts.

NoelOrDie Tumblr fansite

My Noel Gallagher fan blog.

This is a fan blog I created back in June of 2014. I've been using it to post and reblog photos, videos, music, quotes, etc. to keep followers updated on British rock star Noel Gallagher, the lead guitarist and chief songwriter of the band Oasis, who is now a solo artist with his band Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds.

The name NoelOrDie comes from a sort of inside joke within the Noel Gallagher fandom: on Oasis’s final world tour, there was a girl in the crowd in Japan who made a sign that said “NOEL OR DIE!” and Noel seemed quite perplexed by it and thus mentioned it frequently in his tour diaries.

My blog currently has over 2,000 followers, and I update it nearly every day.

NoelOrDie on Instagram

My Noel Gallagher Instagram account.

After the success of my NoelOrDie Tumblr blog, I decided to make an Instagram account in conjunction with the blog to keep fans updated on Noel Gallagher. I try to post something on there at least every day, but the account is most active during the times when Noel is on tour.

My Instagram currently has over 1,300 followers, with new Instagram users discovering and following the account every day.

Noel Gallagher HQ

Noel Gallagher news and updates fansite.

Although my NoelOrDie Tumblr blog is used to post updates and news about Noel Gallagher, I've also used it to post and reblog basically anything and everything circulating on Tumblr that has to do with Noel Gallagher. This means that, although I do post updates, they often get bogged down by all the other Noel-related posts I make. Since there really isn't a fansite dedicated to Noel Gallagher anywhere else on the Web, I decided to take the initiative and make one myself. This blog is now used only to post the latest news and updates on Noel, as well as keep fans updated with his upcoming tour dates and music releases.

Instead of using the usual Tumblr format for Noel Gallagher HQ, I purchased a separate domain name to make the site more accessable to those who do not have a Tumblr account. Although it's still relatively new, Noel Gallagher HQ has nearly 100 followers, with that number growing more and more every day.

NG 2015 Birthday Scrapbook

Noel Gallagher fan project from 2015.

Back in early 2015 when Noel Gallagher first announced his North American tour in support of recent album Chasing Yesterday, it just so happened that the Chicago date I’d been planning to go to fell on his 48th birthday (May 29th). Since I wanted to get him something nice, I decided I’d blast social media asking for “happy birthday” messages from his fans, then put all of them together into a huge scrapbook and give it to Noel at the Chicago show. I made an e-mail account for fans to send their messages to, and I also took messages via Tumblr, Instagram, and Twitter.

Overall, the scrapbook contained 40 pages with 155 messages (not including fan art) that had been sent in from all around the world. The project was most definitely a success, with Noel using his Instagram (themightyi) to take a photo of the scrapbook and say thank you to everyone who was involved!

NG 2016 Fan Survey

Noel Gallagher fan project from 2016.

After the success of the birthday scrapbook in 2015, I decided to create a Noel Gallagher fan survey with the idea that I would collect everyone’s answers and put them together into a book as a gift to Noel on his 2016 tour as a "thank you" and "we love you" present from his fans.

Currently, there are over 170 submissions to the survey, and I'm in the process of going through them, printing them out, and compiling them all into the scrapbook I plan to give Noel Gallagher this July.
